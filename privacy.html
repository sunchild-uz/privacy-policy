SunChild Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

This SunChild Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "the Privacy Policy" or "this Privacy Policy") applies to you personally and your use of the SunChild, which tracks and analyzes caregiver and child data to provide users with needed consultation and Ages and Stages Questionnaire services.

As the data controller, SunChild and affiliates (together, "SunChild", "we" or "us") understand the importance of your privacy. Please take the time to read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we may collect, use and share your personal information in connection with our app.

This app is intended for use ONLY by adults.

1. What Data Do We Collect About You?

We collect and process the following data:

  • User login information (login and password)
  • Caregiver Card: name, surname, relationship to child
  • Child Card: name, surname, DOB, gender, prematurity status (viewable only by doctors)
  • Contact info: address, city, region, country, email, ZIP code, phone number
  • Consultation data: problem description and doctor suggestions
  • Device/network info: IP address, device model, OS, MAC, etc.
  • App usage data: screen views, buttons pressed, features used
  • Customer service communications
  • Online activity info across devices and services

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

We process your data for:

  • Providing SunChild services (consultation, ASQ, support)
  • Analysis and improvement of services
  • Customer communication and support
  • Evaluation and enhancement of app features
  • Compliance with legal requirements

3. How Do We Share Your Data?

Data is shared ONLY with doctors for:

  • Selecting the appropriate ASQ version
  • Consulting on child development and well-being
